background

    I'm an independent consultant, doing visitor studies and evaluation for museums and libraries. My focus is in outcomes-based evaluation and building evaluation capacity within organizations. My goal with each client is to inform decision-making and inspire creativity through excellent evaluation.

    I was trained in evaluation and museum/library studies at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I graduated with a Master's degree, emphasis in community informatics and evaluation. My first official museum job was as Evaluation and Grants Manager at the Ann Arbor Hands-On Museum in 2008. Before that, I had worked in program development, writing, and marketing.

    I currently live in and work out of the San Francisco Bay Area.

recent clients + projects

  • Crocker Art Museum. Sacramento, California. Evaluation consultant & trainer.

  • Troy Public Library. Troy, Michigan. Strategic planning facilitator.

  • Chelsea District Library. Chelsea, Michigan. Strategic planning and evaluation consultant.

  • Ann Arbor Hands-On Museum. Ann Arbor, Michigan. Grant manager for IMLS National Leadership Planning Grant and evaluation consultant.
