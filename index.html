Focusing on outcomes

Outcomes are the changes that happen in people, as a result of what an organization does. Focusing on outcomes keeps organizations relevant.

Asking the right questions

The right evaluation questions begin with internal consensus on critical decisions that need to be made. The right questions can fill our information gaps and check our assumptions.

Facilitating people + ideas

Planning and evaluation are tools for bringing people and ideas together into shared frameworks for decision-making.
